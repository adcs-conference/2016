Welcome

The 21st Australasian Document Computing Symposium (ADCS 2016) will be held in December, 2016.

Aim of the symposium

ADCS 2016 is an opportunity for researchers and practitioners in document management and information retrieval to meet and present their work. The symposium aims to cover all aspects of Document Computing and Information Retrieval. The symposium emphasises both commercial and academic issues by encouraging a variety of submissions.
